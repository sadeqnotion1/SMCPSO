# Starter Deck Outline (L1_overview)

## Audience
Leadership, advisory, and non-technical stakeholders

## Target
- Slides: 5 to 15
- Duration: 10 to 20 minutes

## Objective
Communicate value, outcomes, and next decisions with minimal technical depth.

## Suggested Sections
- Context and motivation
- Problem statement
- High-level solution
- Key outcomes
- Decisions and roadmap
