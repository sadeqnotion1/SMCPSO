# L1 Overview

Use this level for short, high-level presentations.

## Audience

- leadership
- cross-functional teams
- non-technical stakeholders

## Target Length

- 5 to 15 slides
- 10 to 20 minutes

## Focus

- problem statement
- solution summary
- outcomes and impact
- roadmap and decisions

## Material Checklist

- `slides/`: concise narrative deck
- `speaker_notes/`: short talking points per slide
- `figures/`: simple visuals and metrics charts
- `handouts/`: one-page summary
