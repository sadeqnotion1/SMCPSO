# L4 Research Defense

Use this level for thesis defense or full conference-grade technical presentation.

## Audience

- thesis committee
- peer reviewers
- advanced research audience

## Target Length

- 80 to 200+ slides
- 2 to 6+ hours

## Focus

- full methodology and derivations
- experiment design and reproducibility
- statistical evidence and ablations
- limitations, threats to validity, and future work

## Material Checklist

- `slides/`: complete defense deck
- `speaker_notes/`: full script with Q and A prep
- `figures/`: publication-quality plots and tables
- `code_snippets/`: proof-of-method implementation pieces
- `handouts/`: defense appendix pack
- `references/`: complete citation set
